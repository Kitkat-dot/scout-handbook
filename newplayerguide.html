A new player guide to peak!

Peak is generally a game about failing and improving on each consecutive session the goal of your expedition may differ but will always result in you attempting to reach the top of the mountain otherwise known as the peak.

The game does help the player by leaving hints and nudges in the right direction via hints and items for example the players can find the scout handbook which is located within the crash site at the start of the game, I highly advise giving it a read through at the start of the run to familierise yourself with peaks many mechanics and challenges.

Once the player starts a new game they will find themself located within the games hub "The Airport" the player can customise the appeareance of their scout here aswell as test out the games mechanics and physics by climbing there surroundings especially the main rock climbing wall, this is also the area you can invite your friends before starting. The player will then navigate through the hub and find themself at the terminal which will allow the player to select the difficulty of there expedition and set off.

The game begins once the player crash lands on the "shores" biome your main objective once crash landing is too loot any luggage, food or items you may come across in your journey to the top of the mountain. The player will be tasked with climbing through a variety of unique and difficult biomes before eventually lighting a flare to escape the island at the peak.

Status Effects

Peak has a variety of different status effects both positive and negative which will effect the players stamina bar in various ways.

Weight is gained when carrying items and other players who are down and will not deplete over time, the player can reduce weight by using or dropping items/scouts but once picked up again the player will regain the weight. certain items can reduce player weight such as the items anti rope, balloon & balloon bunch.

Hunger is gained over time while the player is idle and climbing it will slowly reduce the players stamina unless the player eats a food item once consumed the hunger bar will be reduced greatly or slightly depending on the quality of the food consumed such as cooked food or special food.

Injury appears on the players stamina bar when there scout takes any form of damage from falls or other sources it will not deplete over time and will only ever be removed once the player uses a healing item, much like food the injury removed will greatly depend on the item used such as the Bandage only healing a small amount compared to the Medkits full health.

Poison is gained when the player touches or interacts with poisonous wildlife or food it can be extremely dangerous as it can easily stack with itself and other debuffs, poison will be removed overtime slowly after 30s have passed with the player taking no poison damage it can also be reduced with items such as Medicinal Root, First Aid kit, Antidote, Remedy Fungus and mythical items such as Cure All, Faerie Lantern and Pandora's Lunchbox

Cold is gained when the player is caught within blizzards in the alpine biome aswell as the fog, Cold will go away on its own there is also items to reduce its effect such as the Lantern or Faerie Lantern Heatpack and Cure-All.

Heat is gained when the player is burnd or in direct sunlight within the mesa biome it will reduce overtime and can be reduced by using items like the Sports Drink, Energy Drink, Antidote, Faerite Lantern, Cure-All Winterberry or Prickleberry, Half-Coconut, Aloe Vera and Pandora's Lunchbox

Thorns is gained within the mesa biome apon touching various cacti or getting hit by tumbleweed these thorns can be removed by waiting 30s in single player or 120 in multiplayer or another scout can remove them from you.

Drowsy can be gained by drinking the Energy Drink item and eating the Big Lolipop or being shot by the blowgun, Drowsy will slowly go away overtime after 10 seconds have passed.

Curse is gained by being revived by the Ancient Statue or a Scout Effigy it can also be gained by consuming another scout and will not deplete overtime it can only be removed by consuming the Cure-All item or using the Pandora's Lunchbox
