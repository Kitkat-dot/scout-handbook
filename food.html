Mushrooms

Mushroom can spawn in a variet of locations but they are predetermined by the seed, although the same location may spawn a different number of mushrooms (it can also spawn with none) every time a player starts a new run. Mushrooms are considered natural food and are safe to consume while attempting the Naturalist Badge.
